# 🟢 Brand Expression

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/AoM Section_Divider_Green.png" alt="AoM Brand Expression section banner, a line-art icon of a full body figure with its skeleton visible, arms outstretched, on a green background."><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### What is it?

Design where and how your brand is expressed across touchpoints.

### Why it matters

Like the skeleton, this Part gives structure to how decisions show up in the world. It ensures consistency across touchpoints. When this part is strong, decisions are expressed clearly and coherently. When this part is weak, expression becomes fragmented, inconsistent, and harder to recognise or trust.
