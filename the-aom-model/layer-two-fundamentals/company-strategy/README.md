# 🟠 Company Strategy

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/AoM Section_Divider_Orange.png" alt="AoM Company Strategy section banner, a line-art brain icon shown in profile on an orange background."><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### What it is?

Choose what your company will and won’t focus on to win in your chosen market(s).

### Why it matters

Like the brain, this Part coordinates the most important decisions across the organisation. It determines what to focus on and what to ignore. When this part is strong, decisions are clear, consistent, and aligned across the business. When this part is weak, decisions conflict, priorities shift, and different parts of the organisation move in different directions.
